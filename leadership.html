CEO/co-founder|Mike Dugas,

PhD in statistics from UC Davis, began his career at Capital One as a statistician in the credit card business.  Over 13 years, Mike has built 2 analytics departments, started the online business for a large brick and mortar payday lender and consulted with many companies spanning a wide range of consumer finance: banks, auto lenders, installment lenders, collections and leasing companies.  He co-founded and sold Credda, a lease to own company. Mike oversees company needs and overall development ensuring MYCFMG is ready for the future!

COO/co-founder|Alex Shumate,

began his career in restaurants quickly moving to upper management solving store problems. Craving more Alex left the industry to join the consumer financing industry where he helped various companies revenue over $10,000,000 per year. Alex began to build his own customer service team in underwriting process reducing merchant fraud and consumer confusion. Having worked with merchants for over 6 years Alex seen a desperate need to help small business owners overcome this “push out” movement of bigger businesses buying companies out and putting smaller ones out of business. When he approached Mike with the idea they were able to develop My Consumer Finance Management Group. Alex works out in the field with his sales team to “get his hands dirty” so he will never lose the heart of the people who mean so much to him… his merchants.
