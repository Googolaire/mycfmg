Our responsibility to Lenders

It is not only the job of MYCFMG to hold lenders responsible for making special offers to our merchants, but MYCFMG has a responsibility to them as well.

  • All merchants must go through MYCFMG’s underwriting process, and are an approved MYCFMG merchant.

  • All Merchants adhere to MYCFMG’s Policy & Procedures.

  • MYCFMG updates merchants on all issues and news from lenders.

  • MYCFMG train merchant’s staff on how to use financing properly

  • Assist in reducing merchant and consumer confusion and fraud.

  • Terminate merchants who are not adhering to company’s policy and procedures.
