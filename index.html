Small businesses represent 99.7% of all employer firms

So why do lenders offer more attractive deals to the larger businesses?

Brand recognition: because these businesses are well known lenders fight to earn bragging rights

Sales Volume: lenders believe that the large sales volume from these stores will also reflect large finance volume

Procedures and standards: the larger stores have protocol when it comes to financing from how to sale it, when to sell it, and most importantly when not to sell it to reduce consumer fraud as well as merchant fraud
