FAQ

Q. What does MYCFMG require from me to sign up?

  1. Fill out our pre-registration form
  2. Make MYCFMG your liaison between you and all your finance companies
  3. Discuss finance needs with a MYCFMG representative
  4. Allow MYCFMG to add and remove finance companies
  5. Send a monthly report to MYCFMG to create reports to present to lenders

Q. How much does it cost for me to join MYCFMG?


A. We have 0 set up fees and were waiving all monthly fees for the first 1000 members

Q How long does it take to MYCFMG to approve my business?


A. Approval and set up normally take 7-10 business days

Q: I already use financing why would I join your group?

A: MYCFMG is more than a source to find new lenders. With our years of experience, we are able to set your store up to have current lenders wanting to get more business from you. On top of that you are grouped with hundreds of business owners like yourself opening more options by being apart of our management system.

Q: You don’t charge me so how do you make money?

A: We are only able to work with businesses that are approved through our underwriting guidelines. Because of our proprietary systems and regulations lenders want to do business with MYCFMG because we reduce risk& fraud.

Q: Can my store be denied by MYCFMG’s underwriting?

A: Yes, it is possible for stores to be denied by our underwriting team. You are always able to re-apply every 90 days.

Q: Will you cancel any finance companies I am currently working with?

A: We never request a merchant to cancel their finance options right away. We will add our finance option for you to use and ensure it is working in your store. If the option can fully replace a lender then we will request a cancellation after 60 days. If the finance option is different then we will not request a cancellation.

Q: Is it possible for my store to be terminated by MYCFMG?

A: Yes, there are a few reasons why a store can be terminated and if that happens the lender will also terminate their relationship with the merchant. This happens because MYCFMG has determined the merchant is unsafe to do business with.

Q: Do I receive anything, so my customers will know that I am apart of MYCFMG?

A: Yes, every time you use one of our lenders you will attach a card that speaks about how your apart of the group and what that means to them. There is also a place on the back to fill out the terms of the lender to re-iterate your commitment to transparency.
